# Code of Conduct

QS-DMSS is intended to be a respectful, evidence-focused project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and kind.
- Prefer reproducible examples over broad claims.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about risks and defects.
- Keep discussions focused on the work, evidence, and public project goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Trolling, spam, or repeated off-topic disruption.
- Public disclosure of suspected vulnerabilities before maintainers have had a
  reasonable chance to respond.

## Reporting

For security issues, use the process in `SECURITY.md`.

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the repository owner or an
appropriate private channel. Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove disruptive
content and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project.
