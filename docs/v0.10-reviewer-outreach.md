current public package baseline is `qs-dmss==0.10.1`; use the current README,

## Contact Policy

Use public professional channels, existing community spaces, or contact paths
that the reviewer has made public. Do not commit personal email addresses to
the repository. If there is no prior relationship with a named person, prefer a
working-group or community-channel ask and invite someone suitable to self-select.

Send 6-8 direct asks first. Hold the rest as follow-ups. The success target is
three public comments total:

- one on `#105` from numerical methods, nonlinear waves, or scientific Python;
- one on `#99` from HPC/RSE/Slurm site-policy experience;
- one on either issue from packaging, reproducibility, or research-software
  review experience.
